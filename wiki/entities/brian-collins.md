## His branding philosophy (HIS — context, not persona)

- **The three-part model — familiarity + surprise + context:** the three things COLLINS
  works with are **familiarity** (constancy), **surprise** (novelty, always original,
  always on the frontier), and **context** (when and where you are familiar vs. when and
  where you are surprising). ([[wiki/sources/2018-08-15-yt-bgStKKE6RUk]])
- **"Provoke love or hate, never indifference":** provoking strong reactions is a sign the
  work matters — people either loved the work or hated it, and sitting right in the center
  of that is fine because it means people **care**. If work weren't interesting, no one
  would say anything at all. Indifference is the real failure.
  ([[wiki/sources/2018-08-15-yt-bgStKKE6RUk]])
- **"Poetry as strategy":** poetry is reframed as a core strategic discipline — good poetry
  doesn't *describe* an experience, it *is* an experience; a short series of powerful words
  can change how someone sees things. ([[wiki/sources/2018-08-15-yt-bgStKKE6RUk]])
- **"Extraordinary over merely relevant":** rejects the framing that brand work must
  balance authenticity (internal: is this who we are?) against relevance (external: what is
  the world looking for?). The goal is to stay authentic while being
  **remarkable / extraordinary / amazing** rather than merely relevant — "if you're not
  extraordinary, no one's gonna pay attention." ([[wiki/sources/2018-08-15-yt-bgStKKE6RUk]])
- **Potency over timelessness:** work does not have to be **timeless**; a good, potent
  expression made with heart, sincerity, and clarity will resonate. Taste and perception
  change over time (1980s work looked dated 15 years ago and now looks fresh again) —
  "it's all context." ([[wiki/sources/2018-08-15-yt-bgStKKE6RUk]])
- **"Anticipation" (via Ray Eames):** cites Ray Eames — "a designer is like a good host,
  anticipating the needs of their guests." Anticipation is a future-facing value: sensing
  what people might need, like, or want before they know it. COLLINS's job is to get
  clients into the future as quickly as possible, and anticipation is the best value to get
  there. ([[wiki/sources/2018-08-15-yt-bgStKKE6RUk]])
