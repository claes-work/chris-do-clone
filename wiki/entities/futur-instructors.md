# The Futur instructors (recurring non-Chris voices)

> ⚠️ **CONTEXT PAGE — NONE OF THESE PEOPLE ARE THE SUBJECT.** This is a speaker-attribution
> reference for ingest: when these names (or their voices) appear in a video, their statements
> must NOT flow into `persona/voice.md` or `persona/beliefs.md`. Only Chris Do's own attributed
> words train the persona. See also [[jose-caballer]] (separate context page).

## Matthew Encina — creative lead, Blind & Futur (era ~2015–2021)

- Creative director at [[blind]] (Emmy-, Clio-, Webby-winning campaigns), then **Chief Content
  Officer at The Futur**; with Greg Gunn oversaw the agency side circa 2017 [press:
  Motionographer, "The future of The Futur", 2017-09-28].
- On camera: role-play demos with Ben Burns, project breakdowns, course content.
- **Left 2021-02-16** ("I Just Quit the Best Job I ever had",
  matthewencina.medium.com/i-just-quit-the-best-job-i-ever-had-60f417c3efb4) [self-reported].
  Later Chief Design Officer at Mode [self-reported: matthewencina.com/about;
  linkedin.com/in/matthewencina].

## Ben Burns — digital director → COO (era ~2016–2021)

- Digital director, then **COO of The Futur**; ran the **Accelerator** coaching program;
  frequent role-play partner opposite Chris Do [press: Motionographer 2017; podcast "LTB 007:
  Mr. Ben Burns, COO of the Futur", podtail.com].
- **Left early Nov 2021** together with coach Stephanie Owens; the Accelerator program spun out
  as **Maker Division** (makerdivision.com); Ben went full-time entrepreneur 2022-02-01
  [self-reported: his LinkedIn/Instagram announcements, linkedin.com/in/mrbenburns; year
  press-uncorroborated — flag `attribution: uncertain` on the exact date]. Also strategic
  advisor at Bravemark (blog.bravemark.co/ben-burns-joins-bravemark-as-strategic-advisor/).

## Greg Gunn — illustrator/animator, creative director (era ~2016–present?)

- Co-founder of studio Three Legged Legs; creative director at [[blind]]; later **Creative
  Director at The Futur** [press-corroborated: motionarray.com/learn/motion-design/interview-greg-gunn/;
  shoutoutla.com profile]. Clients incl. Snapchat, Disney, Microsoft, Starbucks, Riot Games.
- On camera: whiteboard/illustrated explainer videos; author of the "Illustration for
  Designers" course (thefutur.com/shop/illustration-for-designers); illustrated Chris Do's
  book *Pocket Full of Do* (2019).
- Personal: ggunn.com; newsletter "TLDR". Current involvement level with The Futur unverified
  (2026-07-14).

## Melinda Livsey — brand strategist, series co-host (era ~2017–2021)

- Freelance brand strategist, founder of **Marks and Maker** (marksandmaker.com); past design
  work for Oakley, Paramount Pictures, Loot Crate [self-reported: marksandmaker.com/about].
- Pivoted from design to brand strategy in 2017, with Chris Do as a mentor [self-reported:
  UX Design Huddle podcast, 2021, "Melinda Livsey - how to become a brand strategist"].
- On camera: co-host of **"The Process"** series (her business-building journey, on the
  [[thefutur-youtube|YouTube channel]] and podcast) and the brand-strategy 101 role-plays
  with Chris.

## Stephanie Owens — Accelerator coach (era ~2019–2021)

- Coach/portfolio-reviewer in the Futur Accelerator alongside Ben Burns (e.g. "Portfolio
  Review with Ben Burns and Stephanie Owens", YouTube AK2CbuiDbs0); left with Burns in Nov 2021
  to Maker Division [press-corroborated: makerdivision.com; Burns announcements].
