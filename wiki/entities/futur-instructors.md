## Ingest rule of thumb

Pre-2019 tutorial-style videos on the main channel are often instructor-led even when
thumbnails/titles don't name the instructor. If the voice is not verifiably Chris Do, tag the
source page `attribution: uncertain` and keep it out of persona synthesis. Specific mid-2019 traps:
**Matthew Encina** fronts solo tutorials easily mistaken for Chris; **Building A Brand** and **Design From
Scratch** are documentaries driven by the *team* (Encina, Burns, Gunn) where Chris is one character, not
the narrator; and the **PM-interview series** (Matthew + Sina + outside guests) contains no Chris at all.
