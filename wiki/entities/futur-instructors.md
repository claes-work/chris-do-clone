## Matthew Encina — creative lead, Blind & Futur (era ~2015–2021)

- Creative director at [[blind]] (Emmy-, Clio-, Webby-winning campaigns), then **Chief Content
  Officer at The Futur**; with Greg Gunn oversaw the agency side circa 2017 [press:
  Motionographer, "The future of The Futur", 2017-09-28].
- On camera: role-play demos with Ben Burns, project breakdowns, course content.
- **Left 2021-02-16** ("I Just Quit the Best Job I ever had",
  matthewencina.medium.com/i-just-quit-the-best-job-i-ever-had-60f417c3efb4) [self-reported].
  Later Chief Design Officer at Mode [self-reported: matthewencina.com/about;
  linkedin.com/in/matthewencina].
- ⚠️ **Most-confused-with-Chris voice.** Encina fronts many Futur *tutorials* solo and is
  routinely mistaken for Chris Do by ingesters — his teaching must NOT feed the persona. Confirmed
  mid-2019 solo teaches (batches 56–65): the **3-part video-structure framework** (Overview →
  3–5 steps → Outro), self-identifying as "chief content officer" — [[../sources/2019-02-20-yt-VRecjO0A6Ns|Video Content: 3 Steps]];
  the **Adobe/99U creativity-and-productivity recap** (Focus Sprints / Better Brainstorms / Beautiful
  Boredom) — [[../sources/2019-06-14-yt-ybaom7OVtIw|Reclaim your Creativity]]. Both are strong,
  self-contained frameworks flagged L3-for-topics but **barred from `persona/`**.
- ⚠️ **Attribution trap:** the line **"music tells you how to feel"** (with the three-year-old-and-the-movie-score
  anecdote) is **Encina's, not Chris's** — [[../sources/2019-07-24-yt-pRDfov1dbj0|Building A Brand Ep.10]].
  In the *Building A Brand* (Hamilton Family Brewery) and *Design From Scratch* documentaries he presents
  Blind client work as a creative director, not as Chris.

  ([[../sources/2019-07-24-yt-pRDfov1dbj0|Ep.10]]), and the style-guide/reveal finale
  ([[../sources/2019-07-31-yt-pOdBeS43W0A|Ep.11]]). Self-describes as **"champion for the user"**

- **The $100K wrong-barcode QA story is Ben's, not Chris's:** on a past project his team sent packaging

- **On-record money-vs-why tension with Chris (context, not conflict-as-Chris):** the *Design From Scratch*

## Ingest rule of thumb

Pre-2019 tutorial-style videos on the main channel are often instructor-led even when
thumbnails/titles don't name the instructor. If the voice is not verifiably Chris Do, tag the
source page `attribution: uncertain` and keep it out of persona synthesis. Specific mid-2019 traps:
**Matthew Encina** fronts solo tutorials easily mistaken for Chris; **Building A Brand** and **Design From
Scratch** are documentaries driven by the *team* (Encina, Burns, Gunn) where Chris is one character, not
the narrator; and the **PM-interview series** (Matthew + Sina + outside guests) contains no Chris at all.
