## Summary

A first-person "day in the life" vlog documenting a single workday for **Greg Gunn**, creative
director at Blind/The Futur, in Los Angeles (summer 2017). Greg narrates his morning routine
(coffee, journaling to set daily goals on paper), a 30–40 minute commute during which he listens
to podcasts (Gary Vaynerchuk's, Reply All, Science Vs.) or metal/EDM, and his arrival at the
Blind studio around 10am. He plans his day using the To-Doist app, meets with colleagues (Daisy
and others) to review priorities, and takes on a personal project pushed by Chris: digging out
two old college hard drives to build his first portfolio video. Ben Burns helps him pry the
drives out of their cases, but the drives use an outdated interface that none of the studio's
mounts support. The rest of the day fills with back-to-back conference calls, a lunch outing
(Z Garden), a staff meeting, and three meetings in a row — contradicting his earlier claim that
the day would be "chill." He closes by heading home around 7pm to prepare for an early flight to
Seattle the next morning with **Chris and Ben** for a meeting, and to spend the evening with his
wife and dog (Lucy).
