# Design Portfolio Examples - Art Center

## Summary

A short behind-the-scenes / vlog-style clip filming Chris Do on the way to the Art
Center College of Design 2017 Spring Grad Show "Industry Night" event. Chris explains
that he and his studio **Blind** are attending specifically to **recruit** new design
candidates — filling two open slots: a **motion designer** and a **graphic designer**
who "really just understands typography." He frames typography mastery as the single
most important thing he screens for, and clarifies that the real test is not decorative
type but **body type**. He also notes the day involves both a work/portfolio evaluation
and a **cultural-fit** assessment, and observes that top Art Center grads have many
options (Google, YouTube, Facebook, Apple, Amazon are named as competing recruiters),
so a factor is whether candidates are even interested in joining Blind.
