# Wood Cut Effect w/ the Ai Pen Tool — Patrick Seymour

## Summary

A short The Futur feature in which Chris Do interviews Montreal-based illustrator and
art director **Patrick Seymour** about his high-contrast, black-and-white "woodcut"
illustration style built almost entirely in Adobe Illustrator using just the pen tool
and blend tool. Seymour explains his process (reference-driven pencil sketch, then
rebuilding it as sharp vector line-work), his origins as an art director who began
illustrating due to agency budget limits, and how Adobe featured his work on the
Illustrator CC splash screen. Chris Do narrates the intro and closes with a short
career lesson. Low persona value overall — the substance is software mechanics and a
guest's biography.

### Patrick Seymour (guest — CONTEXT, not persona)

  two tools: the pen tool to draw shapes and the blend tool to transition between them.
