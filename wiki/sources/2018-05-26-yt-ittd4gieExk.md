# How Outsiders Break Rules & Create Disruption — Ep. 9 w/ Kier McLaren

## Summary

A short solo talk by Kier McLaren on why outsiders — people new to a field, or coming
from an adjacent one — tend to make the breakthroughs, because they aren't bound by the
established "rules of the road" of a profession. Kier illustrates with personal
anecdotes (writing an action-adventure story with Chris, DJing a country-music show he
knew nothing about), a 1980s book on paradigm shifts, and examples of "outsider"
discoverers (Jonas Salk on polio, Jeff Bezos on bookstores). He closes on giving
yourself permission to fail as a way to break out of your own self-limiting "bubble,"
crediting Chris and other creative people for helping him do so. Chris Do is referenced
throughout but does not appear as a speaker.

- `[2018-05-26]` "Disruptors" are simply people entering an area with a different way of
