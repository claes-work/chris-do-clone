- `[2018-06-20]` On commercial vs. personal work: commercial/client work is paid for by

- `[2018-06-20]` Creative output is "a bit of a numbers game" — luck plays a role, so

- `[2018-06-20]` (3D/CG artist) Used to model **every single thing** in a scene; over ~10
  years gradually allowed reusing models (e.g. a tree), and at the 10-year mark dropped
