## Summary

A The Futur interview in which host Chris Do questions guest Ron Segal about how Ron
earns close to $300k/year working ~30 hours/week as a solo web designer, built largely
on Webflow as his primary leverage. Ron shares three tips: (1) reverse-engineer
award-winning Webflow sites by opening them in the Webflow editor; (2) build a synced
style-guide/starter file (Sketch + Webflow) so global color/type changes cascade;
(3) sell clients and skeptical in-house developers on Webflow via recorded demos (Loom)
and code export. Ron also promotes his $497 Webflow master class (8 modules). Chris's
role is interviewer — probing pricing (project-based/value bidding), the "process over
tools" theme, retainer/hosting business models, and scaling beyond solo work.
