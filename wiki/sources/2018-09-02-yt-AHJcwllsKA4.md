# Traits of Successful People Ep. 19 w/ Keir McLaren

## Summary

Solo talk by Kier McLaren (Chris Do's mentor/coach) enumerating recurring traits he
has observed in the successful creative professionals he has worked with and coached
over his career. Drawing on his time at the motion-design studio Prologue (under Kyle
Cooper), Kier walks through traits — infinite curiosity, pride in one's work, deep
research/attention to detail, egoless willingness to learn, hard work, and doing the
work out of genuine love rather than money or obligation. He illustrates each with
examples: Kyle Cooper's exhaustive research for the *Benjamin Button* titles, sound
designer David Van Slyke's obsession with everyday sounds, and Chris Do as an example
of an accomplished (Emmy-winning) person with "no ego" about his own accomplishments.
He closes with a *Wizard of Oz*-flavored point about knowing one's purpose, and uses
Chris/The Futur as a case study: it took Chris "conservatively three years" of failed
attempts to arrive at the teaching format that worked.
