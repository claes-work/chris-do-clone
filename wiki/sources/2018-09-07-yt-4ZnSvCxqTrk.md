# How To Make Clear Goals You Can Achieve

> Note: Transcript is from YouTube auto-captions (no manual captions available for verbatim precision). NO-CAPTIONS

## Summary

A live 1-on-1 coaching demo in which Chris Do walks a guest (addressed as "Ryan")
through converting a vague aspiration into a specific, achievable goal. Chris opens
with the SMART framework (Specific, Measurable, Actionable, Realistic, Time-bound),
then argues that the three that matter most are **specific, measurable, and
time-bound**. He deliberately elicits a vague answer from Ryan ("financial security,"
"one year from now"), flags it as too vague, and then drills it down in real time:
financial security → "sub 10k a month" → 120k/year → which client mix realistically
gets you there (10 clients × 1k vs. 2 clients × 5k). Chris frames his whole method as
**"chunking"**: taking a big thing and chopping it into a bite-sized piece before
marshalling energy toward it. He closes with a creative-process point: think and gain
clarity first, be objective, look at the whole picture, and **leave the tactical work
to the very end** — resisting the urge to "go squirrel" and jump straight to action
steps.
