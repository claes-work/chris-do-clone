# 1 Simple Tip to Make Your Logos More Valuable

> Speaker: Chris Do (sole speaker — short solo tip video, first person throughout).

## Summary

Chris Do shares a single presentation tip for making logo work look more
valuable and attract bigger clients: place the logo/mark in real-world
context (mockups) rather than showing it flat. Showing a mark on a t-shirt,
in gold foil, on signage, or as wayfinding makes the work read as a
big-budget brand and makes it "very believable." He frames this as useful
both for actual client work and for portfolio pieces, walking through a few
example applications (Dominion Energy, and another mark he references but is
unsure of the exact client name).

### Chris Do

- `[2018-09-28]` (paraphrase) A top tip for designers struggling to find

- `[2018-09-28]` (paraphrase) Seeing a mark in context (on a t-shirt, gold
