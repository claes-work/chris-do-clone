# How do you Communicate What You do? – 1 Minute Tip

## Summary

A short "1 Minute Tip" from The Futur in which Chris Do answers a team's question:
they know who they are and what they want to do, but how do they communicate it in a
single sentence? Chris reframes the exercise away from self-description and toward
customer value: write your mission statement by profiling your customers, identifying
the pain points you can solve for them, and building your messaging from that. Speaker
