# Manage Time Better: A Challenge to Help You Find More Time

**Source:** The Futur (YouTube), 2018-11-08 · [watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0IPDtWmYWVg)
**Speaker:** Chris Do (solo host). Part of the recurring weekly "Commute Challenge" series.

## Summary

A short episode in The Futur's "Commute Challenge" series — a weekly format that gives
viewers quick, actionable challenges to "grow a better business and live a better life."
This week's theme is time. Chris opens with the familiar creative-entrepreneur problem:
7 o'clock rolls around and you look back on the day with no idea what you actually got
done. He frames this as specific to creatives/designers/makers: makers can hold up a
finished thing at day's end, but as you take on entrepreneur/manager roles the day fills
with business and management work that leaves nothing tangible to show. Compounding this
are recurring "time sucks" that drain time and energy — for Chris personally, the biggest
one is email, where he can lose hours without realizing it.

His remedy (and the challenge): pick two separate days within the next seven and track
every single thing you do, using a Google Sheet / spreadsheet in 15-minute increments.
He acknowledges it takes real effort but promises the payoff is clarity on how you're
actually spending your time. He offers an incentive: if 200 people comment committing to
the challenge, he'll share his own daily time log in a follow-up episode, and invites
viewers to post their logs on Instagram with the hashtag #futurcommutechallenge and tag
him. Closes with his signature "go out there and crush it."
