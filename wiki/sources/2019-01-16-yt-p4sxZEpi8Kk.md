# 2 Minute Brain Training Exercise – Unlocking Your Unlimited Potential

## Summary

A short "challenge of the week" segment presenting a mental-reframing exercise called **"limiting beliefs / unlocking unlimited potential."** The host takes words that are painful to artists and creative types — like **NO** and **FAIL** — and reinterprets them as empowering acronyms, stripping the negative connotation from the word. The two worked examples: **NO → "Next Opportunity"** (one door closes, a new door opens) and **FAIL → "First Attempt In Learning"** (you are bound to be bad at anything new, and that is the beauty of learning and growing). The only rule of the exercise is that you cannot use the word itself inside its own acronym.
