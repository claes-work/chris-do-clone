## Summary

Guest business coach Errol Gerson tells a single extended story about gratitude from his years
teaching at Art Center. A student confronts him, calling his emphasis on gratitude "religious
BS" and declaring he is "grateful to nobody for anything." Gerson challenges him with a small
exercise: each morning, thank his bed for a good night's rest. The student resists for days,
then finally does it — and the ripple effect (his girlfriend overhearing, an emotional
six-hour conversation, reconciling with his estranged father after 12 years) becomes Gerson's
illustration that a tiny daily gratitude practice can transform relationships and a life. The
video ends with an editor's note that this is a short cut from a longer one-hour podcast
