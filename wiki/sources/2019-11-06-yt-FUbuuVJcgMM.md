## Summary

A promotional trailer for **Color for Creatives**, an online course by animator/director
**Greg Gunn** sold through The Futur. Greg pitches the course as the fix for creatives
who feel "paralyzed" choosing color: it teaches how to look at and think about color,
build palettes, find and use reference, and learn "the language of color." He lists what
the course includes (multi-part video lecture series, practice assignments, extra color
resources, personal tips/tricks) and closes with a limited-time pre-sale offer — $50 off
for "long-time supporter[s] and friend[s] of the Futur" while the course is being
finished.

Speaker: single-speaker monologue by **Greg Gunn** (not the subject). No Chris Do, no
