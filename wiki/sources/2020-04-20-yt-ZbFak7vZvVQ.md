## Summary

A short Futur workshop clip (part of an Instagram breakdown series introduced by team
member Ricky) arguing that creators should adopt each new social-platform feature the
moment it ships, because platforms tune their algorithms to reward whatever they're
trying to promote. Instagram carousels (recently expanded from ~3 to 10 slides) are the
worked example. The talk closes with a "big three" thesis — YouTube (Google), Instagram
(Facebook), LinkedIn (Microsoft) are the deep-pocketed platforms worth betting on — and
the observation that platform competition benefits creators, who now hold leverage.
