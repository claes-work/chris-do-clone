## The elevator-pitch formula (template)

A three-part fill-in-the-blank structure, delivered in under 30 seconds:

1. **"You know how…"** — state the *problem* your audience/client has.
2. **"I solve that…" / "We solve that…"** (use "we" if you represent a company) — state your *solution*.
3. **"Here's the proof…"** — give *examples / results* that back it up.

The Futur's own worked example (given in the clip as the model answer):
> "You know how creatives undervalue themselves [and] have a hard time communicating their value to others? We solve that by teaching them to speak the language of business so that they become bilingual. Today we have had thousands of successful students, with some doubling and even tripling their income."
