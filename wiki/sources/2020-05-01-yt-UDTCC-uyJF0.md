## Summary

Promo trailer for *Building a Brand*, a Blind/Futur design-documentary series that films
a real rebrand end to end. Season 1 follows the rebrand of **Hamilton Family Brewery**
(Rancho Cucamonga, CA), owned by **Josh and Christine Hamilton**, with **Ben Burns**
(digital director / brand strategist at Blind) and **Matthew Encina** (creative director
at Blind) leading the design team. The trailer sets up the stakes — a small brewery that
needs packaging and a brand strong enough to compete on the shelf — and the tension of a
first-time beer/alcohol branding project.

Speaker: **Multi-speaker promo montage — Chris Do is absent.** Voices are Josh Hamilton
(owner), Christine Hamilton (co-owner, had done the design herself until now), Ben Burns
(Blind digital director / brand strategist), and Matthew Encina (Blind creative director;
caption garbles his name as "Matthew NCN" and "blind" as "Bryan"). Opening voice-over is
unattributed.
