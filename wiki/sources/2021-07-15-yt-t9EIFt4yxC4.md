## Key claims (2021-07-15, paraphrased unless quoted)

**Chris Do (trainable — the subject):**
- The ninth proclamation is "address issues of money early." Resist over-investing in the buying cycle only to
  find out the client can't afford you — this has happened to every single person on the call. We avoid it
  because we're afraid of the tension it creates. *(Chris framing — same as original ~L11–30)*
- Declare your MLE (Minimum Level of Engagement) early in conversation so that, if the client can't afford you,
  both sides can walk away before wasting valuable time and resources. *(Chris, framing Blair's term)*
- There's nothing wrong with telling prospects "we're not for everybody" — firms already say they only work with
  certain types of clients / certain types of work, so why not certain types of financial engagements, over a
  certain minimum level? *(Chris — same as original)*

**Blair Enns (context / do-not-train — WWPM & Pricing Creativity author):**
- "The Win Without Pitching rule of money: those who don't talk about it don't make it." Don't confuse personal-life
  money etiquette with business — in business, an inability to talk money at the right time signals poor business
  acumen. *(Blair, context)*
- MLE starting point = **10% of your annual fee target** (a healthy firm has ~8–15, call it 10, clients).
  $200k target → $20k MLE; want a $400k firm → raise MLE to $40k. State it, then embrace silence. *(Blair, context)*
- The "five constraints / Blairtopia" cap of 10 clients: a hard constraint feels impossible, then survivable,
  then becomes the source of the breakthrough — it forces the MLE conversation and forces you to drop your
  least-profitable client to add a better one. *(Blair, context)*
- Two buyer types: price buyers (want the cheapest) vs. value buyers. "When you exude confidence over money, you
  are exuding confidence over results — the best clients pay a lot for results, the worst pay by the hour for the
  thing." *(Blair, context)*

cutdown contains. Caption note: "mlo" = MLE (Minimum Level of Engagement); "blairtopia" as spoken._
