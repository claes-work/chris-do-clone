## Key claims

### Chris Do (host — PERSONA-relevant)

All dated 2022-09-01 (paraphrase unless quoted):

- When hiring, look for **the things that are difficult or impossible to train** — a
  person who shares your work ethic, drive, intelligence, and values — and train up
  everything else (the skills, the technical how-tos). Hire for **cultural fit / values,
  not just skill.**
- The traits worth screening for: an **open, curious mind**; a **positive, optimistic
  state**; a hunger to learn. If you find that kind of person, they'll likely work out
  long term.
- Business/parenting parallel (Chris's philosophy): just as you should set up your
  children to eventually become their own person — where holding on becomes unhealthy —
  **you want to set up the people you're responsible for in your business so they can go
  out and be their own thing.** (Jokes about his own "three pillars of parenting:
  permission, performance, perseverance.")

### Joana Galvao (guest — CONTEXT, do NOT train persona)

Dated 2022-09-01 (paraphrase):

- Took a ~four-month maternity leave; the transition into motherhood felt "like being hit
  by a bus," and she questioned whether the business would have to run without her. She
  checked in periodically with ops manager Joanna, who protected her time.
- "Patience... fire fast, hire slow" — it's worth waiting for the right person. (NB: she
  states the adage as "fire fast, hire slow"; the conventional phrasing is "hire slow,
  fire fast.")
- Her ops manager Joanna knew nothing about development languages and had to be taught
  everything, but had the right attitude and direct personality — proof of hiring for
  attitude/values over skill.
- Grew **profit, not revenue**: kept revenue roughly flat while drastically increasing
  profit; built savings to cover a full year of team salaries. The formative event was
  2017, when overspending forced her to fire three people in one day over payroll — after
  which she prioritized profit and team mental well-being (wants to be home by 5 for her
  son).
