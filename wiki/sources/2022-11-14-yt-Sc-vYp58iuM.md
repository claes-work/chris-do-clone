# What Clients Do UX Designers Work With?

## Summary

Short Futur **UX-course** clip (interview format) describing the typical clients of a
freelance UX designer.

**Speaker: the unnamed female Futur UX-course instructor** (same series; name not stated),
answering an off-camera question. **Not Chris Do.**

> ⚠️ **CONTEXT / DO-NOT-TRAIN.** UX-course instructor material; does not feed the persona.

## Key claims

Dated 2022-11-14, paraphrased, attributed to the UX instructor.

- The typical client is a **startup entrepreneur** launching a product, online store, or app,
  who hires a freelancer because the budget is lower than an internal hire.
- A growing second group is **design-centered businesses** hiring freelance/contract UX
  designers instead of full-time employees — bigger budgets, some prior guidance, but still
  looking to the designer to guide the direction.
- Both types "know enough to know they need you" but not enough to guide you well.
